## Troubleshooting

| Masalah | Solusi |
|---------|--------|
| QR tidak muncul | Refresh halaman, tunggu 5-10 detik |
| QR expired | Klik "Refresh QR" untuk generate baru |
| Gagal connect | Update WhatsApp ke versi terbaru |
| Sering disconnect | Cek koneksi internet HP, disable battery saver |
| Device logout sendiri | Jangan link ke device lain, cek linked devices di HP |
