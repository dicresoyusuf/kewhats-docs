## General

### What is KeWhats?

KeWhats is a WhatsApp Gateway platform that allows you to send and receive WhatsApp messages programmatically, complete with inbox, broadcast, chatbot, and AI features.

### Is KeWhats official WhatsApp?

KeWhats uses the WhatsApp Web protocol (Baileys library). It's not the official WhatsApp Business API, but more flexible and doesn't require approval from Meta.

### Can my account get banned?

There's a risk if you violate WhatsApp Terms of Service (spam, scam, etc.). Tips to avoid:
- Don't spam
- Use delay between messages
- Only send to consented contacts
- Don't send illegal content

## Device & Connection

### How many devices can I connect?

Depends on your plan:
- Free: 1 device
- Pro: 5 devices
- Enterprise: Unlimited

### Why does my device keep disconnecting?

Common causes:
1. Unstable phone internet connection
2. WhatsApp logged out on phone
3. Linked devices removed from phone
4. Battery saver stopping WhatsApp

### Does my phone need to stay online?

Yes, the phone with WhatsApp must remain online and connected to internet.

---

## Messaging

### How many messages can I send per day?

Recommended max 200-300 messages/day per number with 10-30 second delay.

### Why are my messages failing to send?

Possible reasons:
1. Invalid destination number
2. Number doesn't have WhatsApp
3. Device disconnected
4. WhatsApp rate limit
